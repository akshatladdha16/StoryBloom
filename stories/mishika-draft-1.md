# Mishika and the Moon Garden Mission

**Age Range:** 5-7  
**Theme:** Friendship, imagination, teamwork, and brave dreaming  
**Format:** Rhyming picture-book draft

## Page 1
Mishika packed her crayons bright,
Pink and gold and silver light.
She twirled and said, "Come on, let's go!
The garden has a moonbeam glow!"

## Page 2
Ayushi ran with skipping feet,
Priya brought juice, cool and sweet.
Chandani waved a happy hand,
"Today we build a space girl band!"

## Page 3
They spread a mat beneath a tree,
"Mission Control!" cried one, "That's me!"
They drew a rocket, tall and wide,
With sparkly windows on each side.

## Page 4
Mishika drew in careful lines:
A helmet, stars, and orbit signs.
"I'll be an astronaut one day.
I'll zoom through night and find my way!"

## Page 5
Then out came dolls in dresses neat,
With tiny boots and silver seat.
"My Barbie crew!" Mishika cheered.
"Captain Luna has no fear!"

## Page 6
Ayushi made a cardboard door,
Priya counted, "Three, two, four -
Oops! Let's try that once again!"
Chandani laughed, "Now count to ten!"

## Page 7
"Three, two, one!" they all called loud.
A breeze whooshed soft behind a cloud.
Leaves spun round like stars in flight -
Their rocket garden felt just right.

## Page 8
But then the map flew far away!
Their "Moon Garden" drifted astray.
Mishika paused. "Don't cry, don't race.
We solve things best when we share space."

## Page 9
Ayushi checked near marigold rows,
Priya searched where mint leaf grows.
Chandani looked by puddles small -
Mishika found it by the wall!

## Page 10
They taped the map and drew once more:
A moonlit lake, a crater shore,
A forest dome with glowing trees,
And dancing stars on cosmic breeze.

## Page 11
At sunset, orange filled the sky.
A silver moon climbed soft and high.
Mishika whispered, brave and true,
"One day I'll wave from space to you."

## Page 12
Her friends all hugged her, warm and tight:
"We'll cheer you on each launch at night!
Dream big, draw bright, be kind, explore -
And if one map flies, we'll make one more!"

---

## Illustration Notes
- Keep scenes bright, playful, and nature-rich.
- Show Mishika's drawings and dolls as active parts of the adventure.
- Emphasize friendship expressions: teamwork, encouragement, celebration.
- End with a warm moonlit scene to reinforce hope and aspiration.
